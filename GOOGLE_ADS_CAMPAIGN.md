## Pro Tips for Success

1. **Start with exact match keywords** - More expensive but higher quality
2. **Add phrase match after 1 week** - Once you understand search intent
3. **Broad match = waste** - Don't use it with this budget
4. **Search terms report is gold** - Check it daily, add negatives
5. **Pause ads at night** if performance is poor after hours
6. **A/B test headlines** - Swap out low performers weekly
7. **Conversion tracking issues?** - Check in first 24 hours
8. **Quality Score matters** - Aim for 7+ (improves over time)
